Cosmopolitan

The Cosmopolitan is a colorful drink for a vibrant person. This cocktail is fruity, delicious, and packs a punch. It's the perfect drink for any occasion.

SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 16, 2017

Utilizing fruits and juices, the Cosmopolitan relies heaviy on them for its taste. The ratio of juice to alcohol has to be just right for it to be a true Cosmo. But when its made right, there's no denying just how good this drink really is.

Ingredients

  • Drinking Glass
  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Strainer
  • Vodka
  • Cointreau
  • Lime Syrup
  • Cranberry Juice
  • Orange/Lime
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Get a glass and a shaker
  2. Place the glass in a freezer to chill
  3. In the shaker, pour 1 1/2 ounces of vodka, 1/2 ounce of Cointreau, a splash of lime syrup, 1 ounce of cranberry juice
  4. Fill the shaker with ice and shake for about 30 seconds
  5. Take the chilled glass out of the freezer
  6. Using the strainer, pour the mixture into the glass
  7. Garnish with a lime slice or an orange rind
  8. Enjoy!

Tasteful Tip:

A Cosmopolitan is best served in a cocktail glass.
