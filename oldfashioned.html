Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned is exactly what it sounds like; a classic. This cocktail, simple, yet delicious, will surely satisfy anyone looking for a whiskey treat.

SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 10, 2017

Being a whiskey-based cocktail, the Old Fashioned focuses mostly on its relationship between sugars and bitters. The conflicting ingredients balance themselves out, while seamlessly enhancing the other flavors in the drink, giving a truly timeless taste.

Ingredients

  • Drinking Glass
  • Spoon
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Bitters
  • Bourbon
  • Club Soda
  • Cherries
  • Oranges
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Get a glass
  2. Pour 1 tablespoon of sugar, 2-3 dashes of bitters, a few drops of water, and 2 ounces of bourbon into the glass
  3. Stir until the sugar is dissolved
  4. Add as much ice as you would like to the drink
  5. Fill the rest of the glass with club soda
  6. Stir until the drink is chilled
  7. Garnish with a cherry or orange wheel
  8. Enjoy!

Tasteful Tip:

The Old Fashioned is best served in a rocks glass.
