Green Melon Mojito

The Green Melon Mojito is an experience in a drink. This cocktail, a twist on a common classic, is delicious in its own right. This is because it builds upon a flavorful favorite with the addition of melon, truly putting it over the top.

TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 19, 2017

Revolving around ingredients that require a little more effort to be made, the Green Melon Mojito really depends on its execution. If made right, the mixture will blend together seamlessly and taste delicious. Creating this drink may seem like a daunting task at first, but it is surely worth it.

Ingredients

  • Drinking Glass
  • Cocktail Shaker
  • Small Saucepan
  • Ginger Simple Syrup (See "Steps" for Instructions)
  • Blender
  • Cantaloupe Puree (See "Steps" for Instructions)
  • Strainer
  • White Rum
  • Lime
  • Seltzer
  • Mint
  • Ice

Steps

  1. First, get a small saucepan to begin creating the ginger simple syrup
  2. In the saucepan, heat up equal parts of water and sugar and a few small chunks of ginger on medium-low temperature until the sugar is completely dissolved
  3. Let it cool and remove the ginger
  4. Next, get a blender for the cantaloupe puree
  5. In the blender, mix cantaloupe and a little bit of seltzer until it is completely liquified
  6. Once these steps are complete, grab the shaker
  7. In the shaker, pour 1 ounce of cantaloupe puree, 1 1/2 ounces of white rum, 3/4 ounce of ginger simple syrup, 1/2 of a lime's juice, and 1 1/2 ounces of seltzer
  8. Shake for about 30 seconds
  9. Then, grab the glass and put as much ice as you would like in it
  10. Using the strainer, pour the mixture into the glass
  11. Fill the rest of glass to the top with seltzer and stir
  12. Garnish with mint
  13. Enjoy!

Tasteful Tip:

The Green Melon Mojtio is best served in a highball glass.
